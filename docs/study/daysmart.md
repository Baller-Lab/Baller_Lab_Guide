# DaySmart
  
[DaySmart](https://account.appointment-plus.com/ap/ap_admin_v2/appointments_index_v2.php?p=appts&first=yes) is an online scheduling website that all protocols use. Every appointment that you make, both in-person and virtual, must be added into DaySmart. Once you are on the website, ensure that you are on the Rooms view of DaySmart, with all rooms selected. This will allow you to see what rooms are available for an in-person assessment rooms, and which have already been booked by other coordinators. To schedule an appointment:

1. Click on the calendar in the room and time you want.
    1. If virtual, always book in the virtual column.
    2. If in-person, use one of the colored rooms. 
2. Click `Add Appointment`
3. If adding a new participant, choose `+ Add New Research Participant`
    1. Add the following information: BBL ID, protocol (msdep_k23), and Scheduled by (your first name), then click `Create`.
4. If scheduling a pre-existing participant search for their BBLID in the search bar, and then click on the BBLID once it shows up.
5. Then, you will add assessment types. This is done in three chunks:
    1. First, you will select “Virtual Consent” (if virtual) or “Consent & Intake” (if in-person) in `Assessment Type`
    2. `Assessor` will be you and eballer
    3. `Room` will be the color room or virtual
    4. Make sure the `time` is what you are expecting
    5. `Duration` for consent will be 30 minutes
    6. Then, click `+ Add New Assessment Type`
    7. Repeat these steps:
        1. Chunk two will be “CNB” or “Virtual CNB”
            1. Duration will be two hours
            2. All other information remains the same
        2. Chunk three will be “Self-Report Questionnaires” or “Virtual Self-Report Questionnaires”
            1. Duration will be 30 minutes
            2. All other information remains the same
6. Once done, click `Next` and then schedule the appointment.

### Updating Status in DaySmart

You will also have to update the status of your appointment. It will start off as a light blue color, which means it is “Scheduled”. The following are the other status options:

1. Dark blue: Confirmed
2. Red: No show
3. Purple: Rescheduled-Internal
    1. This is for when the coordinator reschedules
4. Pink: Rescheduled
    1. This is for when the participant reschedules
5. White: Cancelled-Internal
    1. This is for when the coordinator cancels
6. Yellow: Partially Completed
7. Green: Completed
    1. **This is the most important update. You MUST update DaySmart with the completed status within ~24 hours of completing the visit.**

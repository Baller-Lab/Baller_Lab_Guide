# Call Log
The Call Log, located on Saturn, is where we store all of our participants information, including prospective participants and completed visit information. Each tab in this spreadsheet is detailed below. It can be found in: 

Coordinators/Protocols/BALLER LAB/MS_and_Depression_853883/Participant_Recruitment/MS_Dep_Call_Log.xlsx

### List of Potential Participants

Here is where we record all potential participants. Each row includes information about a participant who has either expressed interest in our study or has been referred to us. A yellow participant indicates that they have yet to be reached out to or scheduled. A blue participant is someone who has been scheduled. A grey participant is someone who we’ve reached out to but never responded or said they weren’t interested. A pink participant is someone who you want to eventually come back to (i.e., their first scan is in 3 months from calling them). Make sure to include all contact notes in the Call/Log Notes column. When a participant has completed their visit, turn their row green and then minimize the row (make sure to include the necessary information in the next tab “MS_Dep_Call_Log” beforehand. 

### MS_Dep_Call_Log

After completing a visit, the participants information will be moved over to this tab. Here is where you will record their information and additional notes about what was vs wasn’t completed during the visit, and if they requested feedback. Make sure to keep those tallies updated.

### Excluded Pts

This list includes any participants who completed our study but had to be excluded. Please include the reason for exclusion.

### 7T

This is a list from a collaborating lab (Dr. Schindler) and their recruitment list for a study where the participants receive 7T imaging. Grey = not eligible for our study, green = they’ve completed our protocol, white = contact them, orange = low on priority list. 

### 2 Visits

A sample of participants were asked to come back for a time point 2 visit for data to submit a grant. All have been completed and recorded here.

### Tracker

Here is where we track the numbers. It is set up so that when you correctly fill out the MS_Dep_Call_Log tab, it automatically updates this page. You can see how many people have completed the study, who is interested in being re-contacted for a longitudinal study, race and ethnicity, and NIH recruitment goals. Make sure you are staying on track with these NIH goals. If you successfully complete those goals for the month, highlight it in green. If you didn’t complete them, highlight it in orange.

### Deprioritized

This is where deprioritized participants information can stay. It is not necessary to update this, as you can leave them in the List of Potential Participants tab instead.

### Declined

This is where participants who declined to participate information can stay. It is not necessary to update this, as you can leave them in the List of Potential Participants tab instead.

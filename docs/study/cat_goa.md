# The Computerised Adaptive Version of the GOASSESS (CAT-GOASSESS)
The CAT-GOASSESS is a clinical assessment that provides a dimensional characterisation of lifetime psychopathology. Rather than generating clinical diagnoses, which tend to have overlapping symptoms, the CAT-GOASSESS produces transdiagnostic z-scores for five domains: mood and anxiety, phobias, externalising disorders, psychosis, and personality disorders.

### Administer the CAT-GOASSESS In-Person:
1. Navigate to the [website](https://phenome.med.upenn.edu) and click `Admin Tools`
2. Under `Common Actions`, choose `Administer CAT GOASSESS (without using a preconfigured link)`
3. Then, enter the `BBLID` and choose `GOASSESS CAT Standard Collection` for `Domain`, and then `Begin`
4. You can then turn the computer to the participant and instruct them to take themselves through the assessment

### Administer the CAT-GOASSESS Virtually:
1. Navigate to the [website](https://phenome.med.upenn.edu) and click `Admin Tools`
2. Under `Admin`, choose `Generate/manage participant links`
3. Select `GOASSESS CAT Standard Collection` for `Domain`, enter the `BBLID`, and keep `# of Links` at 1
4. Then, `Generate Links`, copy the link, and paste it in the Zoom chat, and instruct them to take themselves through the assessment

### Downloading GOA Data
You will need to export and save each GOASSESS completed, saving it in both Saturn and Box. To export the GOA data:
1. Begin on the `Admin Tools` home page, and under `Session Data`, select `Participant lookup`
2. Enter the `BBLID`
3. Then, click on the blue hyperlink `YYYY-MM-DD, GOASSESS CAT Standard Collection`
4. Right-click `Export to CSV`, and choose `Download linked file`
5. Save in Saturn: Protocols/BALLER LAB/MS_and_Depression_853883/GOA_Data
6. Save in Box: elena_projects/redcap_goa/data/goa_raw_data

# Penn Institutional Review Board (eIRB)
The Penn Institutional Review Board is housed on [eIRB](https://irb.upenn.edu/homepage/latest-updates/news/eirb/). This study is called Investigating Mood and Cognition in Patients with Multiple Sclerosis (853883). Please refer to this [link](https://irb.upenn.edu/homepage/how-to-submit/) for submission guidelines. Make sure to have tracked and clean copies of everything submitted. Make new folders for every submission where you include all documents submitted and the notice of approval (once received).
